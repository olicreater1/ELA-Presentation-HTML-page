Engineering Lesson Learned #10

There's Always Trade-offs

In engineering, there is no perfect solution. There is always something to be desired. In any solution, some things are desired and others are not. Engineering is all about taking into account what trade-offs to make. In the real world, this is prevalent anywhere. Every decision you make has trade-offs, it is up to you to decide what trade-offs are acceptable to you. This connects to a quote from Ove Arup, quote.
