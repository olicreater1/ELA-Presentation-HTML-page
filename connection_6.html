Engineering Lesson Learned #6

Adapt, Improvise, Overcome

In engineering and real life, you need to be able to adapt, improvise, and overcome to be able to solve problems. Just like how you must do the same in real life when faced with a problem, you must be able to adapt, improvise, and overcome to solve the problem.
