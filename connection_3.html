Engineering Lesson Learned #3

Measure Twice, Cut Once

Material costs are no joke. Make sure you do it right when you can easily make changes before you spend money on material. This applies to engineering with building things, but it also applies to real life when you build or create for a random project and to projects in general. Always make sure you do whatever you need to do correctly and in its best state before finalizing or turning in whatever you worked on.
