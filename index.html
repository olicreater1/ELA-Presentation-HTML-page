Jonathan Ort - How Engineering Is A Metaphor For Real Life

Engineering is a metaphor for life because it shows that when you are faced with a problem there are many solutions and you can choose any of them after weighing what each would do. One of the most important parts of engineering is finding the optimal solution to a problem. This relates to real life because in the real world, when you are faced with a problem many solutions exist but you must weigh the pros and cons of each solution and figure out what's best for you.
