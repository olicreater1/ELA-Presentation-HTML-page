Engineering Lesson Learned #5

Test And Iterate

One of the most important things to do when designing and creating something is to test and make new iterations until the invention is able to do what it needs to do. This connects to real life in how it shows that your first try may not be perfect, but that's why you need to find what you did wrong and try to work on it so you do better next time.
